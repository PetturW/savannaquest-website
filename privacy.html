🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

TL;DR: We don't collect, store, or share any of your data. Everything stays on your device. Period.

Our Privacy Commitment

SavannaQuest is designed with your privacy as the top priority. We believe your safari adventures are yours alone, and we have no interest in tracking, collecting, or profiting from your data.

This privacy policy explains how we handle (or rather, don't handle) your information.

Location Data

SavannaQuest uses your device's GPS to track your safari routes. Here's exactly what happens with your location data:

  • Stored locally only: All GPS data is saved directly on your device in the app's private storage
  • Never transmitted: We never send your location to our servers, third parties, or anyone else
  • You control it: Delete your data anytime through app settings
  • Only when active: Location is only tracked when you explicitly start a safari session
  • No background tracking: We don't track you when the app isn't in active use
  • No geofencing: We don't monitor when you enter or leave specific areas

What We DON'T Collect

Unlike most apps, we deliberately avoid collecting:

  • ❌ No user accounts or email addresses
  • ❌ No analytics or usage tracking
  • ❌ No advertising IDs or tracking pixels
  • ❌ No crash reports with personal data
  • ❌ No device fingerprinting
  • ❌ No third-party data sharing
  • ❌ No social media integration
  • ❌ No cookies or web beacons
  • ❌ No contact list access
  • ❌ No photo metadata collection

Offline Maps

Map tiles are downloaded and stored on your device during app installation. These maps:

  • Work completely offline
  • Require no internet connection to use
  • Don't communicate with any servers
  • Are based on OpenStreetMap data (open source and publicly available)
  • Are stored in the app's private storage area

Map data is licensed under the Open Database License (ODbL).

Permissions We Request

Location Access (Required)

Required to track your safari routes and show your position on the map. We request:

  • "While Using the App" permission on iOS
  • "Precise Location" for accurate GPS tracking
  • Location data is only collected during active safari sessions

Storage Access (Automatic)

Used to save offline maps and your safari route history on your device. No cloud storage or external servers involved.

Photo Library (Future Feature - Optional)

If we add the ability to attach photos to waypoints, you'll be able to optionally grant photo access. This will only be used to select photos you want to attach to specific locations. We will never scan or access your photo library without explicit permission.

Your Data Rights

Since all data stays on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: View all your safari data anytime in the app
  • Export: Future feature to export routes as GPX files
  • Delete: Clear individual safaris or all data through Settings
  • Uninstall: Deleting the app removes all associated data from your device

You don't need to contact us to exercise these rights - you have direct control through the app.

Children's Privacy

SavannaQuest is suitable for all ages. Since we don't collect any personal information, there are no special privacy concerns for children using the app. Parents can review the app's location permissions in device settings.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy practices, we will:

  • Update this page with the new policy
  • Change the "Last updated" date at the top
  • Notify users through an in-app message for significant changes
  • Never start collecting data without making it very clear and getting your consent

We will never make changes that reduce your privacy without explicit opt-in consent.

Third-Party Services

SavannaQuest does not use:

  • Analytics services (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • Advertising networks
  • Crash reporting services that collect personal data
  • Social media SDKs
  • Cloud storage services

The app is completely self-contained and does not communicate with any external services after initial download.

Data Security

Your safari data is protected by your device's built-in security features:

  • Data is stored in the app's sandboxed storage (inaccessible to other apps)
  • iOS/Android encryption protects data at rest
  • Your device passcode/biometric lock protects access

We recommend:

  • Using a strong device passcode or biometric lock
  • Keeping your operating system up to date
  • Being cautious when sharing your device with others

International Use

SavannaQuest is designed for use in African safari parks but can be downloaded worldwide. Since all data stays on your device, there are no cross-border data transfers or international privacy concerns.

GDPR Compliance

While SavannaQuest doesn't collect personal data, we respect GDPR principles:

  • Data Minimization: We collect zero personal data
  • Purpose Limitation: Location is only used for route tracking
  • Storage Limitation: You control how long data is kept
  • Transparency: This policy explains everything clearly
  • User Rights: You have full control over your data

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have the right to know what personal information we collect. The answer is simple: none. We don't collect, sell, or share personal information.
